About JNARI

Journal of National Academic Research and Innovation (JNARI)

Established in 2025, the Journal of National Academic Research and Innovation (JNARI) is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing research in business administration, management, entrepreneurship, and allied disciplines.

JNARI publishes monthly issues in English and follows a rigorous double-blind peer-review process ensuring high academic quality, research integrity, and global accessibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how JNARI supports researchers, authors, and academic institutions through advanced publishing tools and global collaboration.

What is JNARI and who can join?

JNARI is a platform connecting researchers, academicians, and institutions. Membership is open to scholars, students, and professionals seeking academic publishing and research collaboration.

How can I access research resources on JNARI?

Members can access research papers, journals, publication guidelines, and expert consultations directly through the platform.

Can I collaborate with other researchers?

Yes, JNARI enables collaboration by connecting researchers and institutions globally for joint publications, research projects, and academic networking.

How does JNARI ensure ethical publishing standards?

JNARI follows strict peer-review processes, plagiarism checks, and ethical research standards to maintain the quality and credibility of publications.

Are there training or learning resources available?

Yes, JNARI provides webinars, tutorials, and expert sessions to help researchers stay updated with modern research methodologies and publishing standards.

How can I participate in the JNARI community?

Members can join discussions, submit research papers, attend webinars, and collaborate with academic experts worldwide.
