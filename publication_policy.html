Publication Policies

Journal of National Academic Research & Innovation (JNARI) follows ethical and structured publication standards aligned with national ISSN guidelines.

1. Introduction

The Journal of National Academic Research & Innovation (JNARI) is a peer-reviewed scholarly journal dedicated to research in business administration, management, finance, economics, national business, and related disciplines. These publication policies follow ISSN guidelines and COPE standards.

2. Scope of the Journal

  • Original research articles
  • Review papers
  • Case studies
  • Conceptual papers
  • Short communications
  • Book reviews

3. Editorial Policy

  • Editorial independence in decision making
  • Decisions based on academic merit
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Handle corrections and ethical issues

4. Peer Review Policy

Double-Blind Peer Review System

  • Confidential author & reviewer identity
  • Objective and constructive feedback
  • Evaluation based on originality and relevance

5. Author Guidelines & Ethics

  • Significant contribution required for authorship
  • No plagiarism, fabrication, or falsification
  • Ethical research compliance mandatory
  • All authors must approve submission

6. Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts must be in English
  • Structured abstract (150–250 words)
  • Keywords (5–7)
  • Word count: 4000–8000

7. ISSN Compliance

  • Maintain consistent journal identity
  • Display ISSN clearly
  • Ensure regular publication
  • Proper archiving of issues

8. Conflict of Interest

All contributors must disclose financial, professional, or personal conflicts that may influence research outcomes.

9. Open Access & Copyright

  • Gold or Hybrid Open Access model
  • Creative Commons licensing
  • Authors retain copyright

10. Retractions & Corrections

  • Retraction for plagiarism or fake data
  • Corrections for minor errors
  • Withdrawal before acceptance only

11. Archiving Policy

JNARI ensures long-term preservation through institutional repositories and national/international databases.

12. Publication Frequency

Quarterly / Biannual / Annual publication is maintained consistently for ISSN compliance.

13. Advertising Policy

Advertising does not influence editorial decisions. Sponsored content is clearly disclosed.

14. Confidentiality Policy

Manuscripts, reviewer identities, and editorial decisions are kept strictly confidential.

15. Compliance

JNARI follows ISSN guidelines, COPE standards, ICMJE recommendations, and national scholarly publishing best practices.
