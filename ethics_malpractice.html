Ethics & Malpractice Statement

Journal of International Academic Research & Innovation (JIARI) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics and integrity in International scholarly publishing.

Editorial Board

  • Fair and transparent manuscript evaluation based on merit
  • No discrimination (gender, International ty, institution)
  • Maintain strict confidentiality
  • Handle conflicts of interest responsibly
  • Follow COPE guidelines for misconduct

Reviewers

  • Support editorial decision-making
  • Provide objective and constructive reviews
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Declare conflicts of interest
  • Ensure timely review process

Authors

  • Submit original and plagiarism-free work
  • Ensure accurate and honest reporting
  • Provide data transparency
  • Follow proper authorship guidelines
  • Avoid duplicate submissions

Publisher

  • Ensure integrity of scholarly records
  • Handle corrections and retractions
  • Maintain digital archiving systems
  • Follow ethical publishing standards

Malpractice Policy

  • Strict plagiarism detection
  • Reject unethical submissions
  • Retract published misconduct
  • Inform institutions if required
  • Ban authors for serious violations

Compliance

  • Aligned with International ISSN standards
  • Ensures transparency & accountability
  • Promotes ethical research practices
