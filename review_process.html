Review Process

Journal of National Academic Research & Innovation (JNARI) follows a transparent and rigorous peer-review workflow ensuring academic integrity, research quality, and ethical publication practices.

1. Manuscript Submission

  • Full manuscript in required format
  • Abstract, keywords, and author details
  • Originality & copyright declaration
  • Ethical compliance statements

2. Initial Screening

  • Scope and relevance check
  • Formatting compliance
  • Plagiarism verification
  • Document completeness

3. Editor Assignment

  • Subject expert assigned
  • Checks originality & quality
  • Evaluates ethical standards

4. Double-Blind Review

Confidential evaluation process

  • Minimum two reviewers
  • Third reviewer if conflict arises
  • Fair and unbiased review

5. Editorial Decision

  • Accept / Reject
  • Minor or Major Revision
  • Revise & Resubmit

6. Revision Stage

  • Submit revised manuscript
  • Point-by-point response
  • Highlight changes

7. Final Acceptance

  • Editor-in-Chief approval
  • Quality & compliance check
  • Ethical validation

8. Copyediting

  • Language editing
  • Formatting & references
  • Proofreading by authors

9. Online First

Articles are published online before issue release for faster dissemination.

10. Publication

  • Issue assignment
  • ISSN & DOI details
  • Publication date

11. Post-Publication Ethics

  • Corrections & retractions
  • Ethical issue handling
  • COPE compliance
